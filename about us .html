Meet Shronda Jeanine

My journey into healing work began through my own experiences with grief and loss. After navigating profound personal challenges, I discovered that our deepest wounds can become our greatest sources of strength and compassion for others.

What started as my own healing journey evolved into a calling to support others through their darkest moments and guide them toward reclaiming their power. I believe that healing isn't just about moving past trauma—it's about transforming it into wisdom, strength, and authentic living.

Today, I combine professional training in grief counseling and life coaching with intuitive guidance, creating a holistic approach that honors both the practical and spiritual aspects of healing. Whether through one-on-one sessions, nourishing cottage foods, or the simple act of caring for plants, everything I do is rooted in the belief that healing happens in community and through genuine care.
